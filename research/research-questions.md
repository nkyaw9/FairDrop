# Research Questions

## User Experience Questions

- How do users currently experience high-demand online drops such as concert tickets, sneakers, collectibles, and limited-release products?
- What frustrations do users commonly experience during online queues and waiting room systems?
- What causes users to perceive online drop systems as unfair or untrustworthy?
- How do bots and resellers affect user trust and purchasing behavior?
- What emotions do users experience during high-demand online drops?

---

## Fairness & Transparency Questions

- What does “fairness” mean to users in the context of online drops?
- Do users trust current queue and waiting room systems?
- What information would make queue systems feel more transparent?
- Would users prefer first-come-first-serve systems, lotteries, verified access systems, or another approach?
- What tradeoffs are users willing to make for a fairer system (ex: longer wait times, identity verification, lotteries)?

---

## Reseller & Scalping Questions

- How do resale markets affect buyer behavior and purchasing decisions?
- How do users feel about inflated resale pricing?
- What role do users believe resellers play in current online drop systems?
- How do resellers impact the experience of real fans and buyers?

---

## Technical & Infrastructure Questions

- What anti-bot and queue systems are currently being used by platforms?
- What technical limitations exist in current online drop systems?
- How can fairness, scalability, and system performance be balanced during high-traffic releases?
- How do current systems prioritize traffic management versus user trust and transparency?

---

## Product & Business Questions

- Would users trust a new fairness-focused drop infrastructure system?
- What features would make users feel more confident in a platform?
- Would smaller businesses or event organizers benefit from a fairness-focused queue system?
- How might a fairness-focused infrastructure system improve user trust and long-term customer experience?
