# Interview Questions

## Background & Experience

1. Have you ever tried buying concert tickets, sneakers, collectibles, gaming consoles, or other limited-release products online?
2. What was the item or event?
3. Were you able to successfully purchase it?
4. How often do you participate in high-demand online drops?

---

## Current User Experience

5. Walk me through the last time you tried purchasing a high-demand product online.
6. What platform or app did you use?
7. What was the most frustrating part of the experience?
8. Did the process feel fair to you? Why or why not?
9. Did you experience long wait times, crashes, errors, or disappearing inventory?
10. Did you feel bots or resellers had an advantage during the process?

---

## Queue & Waiting Room Systems

11. How do you feel about current queue and waiting room systems?
12. Do you trust how online queues are organized?
13. What information do you wish platforms showed users during queues?
14. Would transparency about queue position or odds improve your trust in the system?
15. Which system feels most fair to you:
   - First come first serve
   - Lottery/randomized queue
   - Verified fan access
   - Reservation system
   - Other

Why?

---

## Resellers & Scalping

16. Have you ever purchased from a reseller?
17. How much more did you pay compared to retail price?
18. How did paying resale prices make you feel?
19. Have you ever skipped an event or product because resale prices became too expensive?
20. What do you consider fair versus unfair reselling?

---

## Trust & Fairness

21. What makes an online drop system feel trustworthy?
22. What makes a system feel unfair or “rigged”?
23. Would you wait longer if it meant the system was more fair?
24. Do you think current platforms prioritize real fans or profit more?

---

## Verification & Anti-Bot Systems

25. Would you verify your phone number if it reduced bots?
26. Would you verify your identity if it created a fairer system?
27. What anti-bot measures would feel reasonable to you?
28. Would purchase limits improve the experience?

---

## Future & Improvements

29. If you could redesign online drops, what would you change?
30. What would an ideal online drop experience look like to you?
31. Is there anything current ticketing or drop systems are doing well?
32. What would make you trust a new platform or fairness-focused system?
